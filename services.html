Service FAQs

How deep can you drill a borehole?

Our drilling equipment can reach depths of up to 300 meters, depending on geological conditions. The actual depth required for your borehole will be determined during our site survey, based on water table levels and geological formations in your area.

What's the typical project timeline?

Most standard borehole projects take between 3-7 days for drilling and casing, with additional time required for pump installation and water testing. Complex projects or those requiring extensive surveys may take longer. We provide a detailed timeline during the proposal stage.

Do you offer maintenance services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive maintenance packages for all water systems we install. Our maintenance services include regular inspections, pump servicing, water quality testing, and system performance optimization. We recommend annual maintenance for optimal system longevity.

What areas do you serve?

We operate throughout Kenya, with extensive experience in Elgeyo Marakwet, Uasin Gishu, Nakuru, and West Pokot counties. We also provide services in South Sudan and Uganda. Contact us to confirm availability in your specific location.
