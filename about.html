About KITANY DRILLERS

KITANY DRILLERS is a premier water drilling company operating across Kenya, South Sudan, and Uganda. With over 15 years of experience in the industry, we are committed to providing sustainable water solutions to communities, businesses, and government institutions throughout East Africa.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, reliable water drilling services while promoting environmental sustainability and community development through innovative water solutions.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and technologically advanced water drilling partner in East Africa, transforming lives through sustainable water access.
