FAQ

Common questions and answers about our drilling services.

What areas do you serve?

We operate in Kenya, parts of South Sudan, and Uganda.

What is your pricing model?

Our pricing is tailored to each project. Contact us for a custom quote.

How long does it take to drill a borehole?

The duration depends on the depth and ground conditions, but typically it takes between 2 to 7 days.

Do you offer maintenance services?

Yes, we provide regular maintenance and repair services to ensure the longevity of your borehole.

What type of equipment do you use?

We use modern and efficient drilling rigs and support equipment to ensure quality and safety during drilling.
