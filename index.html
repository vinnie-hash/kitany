Our Featured Projects

Borehole Drilling in Eldoret

Our comprehensive borehole drilling project in Eldoret provides sustainable access to clean water for hundreds of residents. Using state-of-the-art drilling equipment and techniques, we've established a reliable water source that serves both domestic and agricultural needs. The project includes:

  • • 150m deep borehole with stainless steel casing
  • • Solar-powered pumping system
  • • Water storage and distribution network
  • • Community training on water management

This initiative has significantly improved public health while supporting local agriculture and economic development.

Water Treatment Plant in Nakuru

Our advanced water treatment facility in Nakuru transforms local water sources into safe, potable water for thousands of residents. The plant features:

  • • Multi-stage filtration system
  • • UV and chemical disinfection
  • • 50,000 liter per day capacity
  • • Automated monitoring systems

This state-of-the-art facility has dramatically reduced waterborne diseases in the region while providing a model for sustainable water treatment solutions. The energy-efficient design minimizes operational costs and environmental impact.
